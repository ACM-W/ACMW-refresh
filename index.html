What we do

✻

The Cal State Fullerton chapter of the ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates for the full engagement, education, and opportunities of women in all aspects of the computing field. We endeavor to provide a wide range of programs and services to students and advance, in the larger community, the contributions of technical women students.

Get Involved!

We hope you take the opportunity to unite with our club and further your experience and knowledge!

Our general meetings are on Wednesdays from 5:30 - 6:45 p.m.

We're always looking for new members and any volunteers are always welcomed! No membership fee is required, just your enthusiasm and interest. Thank you!
